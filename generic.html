Summary

Honesty. Creativity. Passion. These personality traits describe me as a person and also relay to employers that I bring more than just intellect to the table. My outgoing personality and honesty give me an edge that other students don't have. I always give each challenge my all, and after I finish the challenge I always make a note to reflect on what I could've done better.

I chose computer science as my challenge and hope to obtain the skills necessary to succeed at one of North America's leading technology centers. Computer science is not just a field of study, it is an art. Everyone in this field has different ways of solving a problem, but which way is the most correct? This has been a debatable subject but I feel like the best solutions to a compute science problem are dynamic. Dynamic solutions are able to adapt to specific consumer needs and are able to compensate for any changes that may occur. Just as in art there are different ways to express ones self, computer science also shares that same sentiment. This is the philosophy I hope to adapt to my work in the real world!
